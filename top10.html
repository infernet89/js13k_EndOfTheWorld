js13kGames TOP10

Here my personal top 10 of js13kGames. They are not in order, see FAQ.

  • Cave Explorer

    Even if there are only two levels, the mechanic is very interesting and the physics of fluid is awesome. Worth your time!

  • Elematter

    This is here probably only because i LOVE tower defense games. This is very pleasurable to see.

  • Rescue Copter

    Amazing graphic style and very fun endless arcade gameplay. And it's really polished.

  • Elemental Block Shooters

    This is fun! The graphic style is very clever and the sounds are perfect for the design. The gameplay is nothing really new, but is fun to be played.

  • Radial

    Usally I'm not a big fan of frantic arcade games, but this is different. The moving pattern actually change after a while, making it a very fun challenge!

  • Pest Control : Weasels

    This is not only a perfect lemmings clone, it actually create a reverted gameplay on it. My only complain is on the level design, a bit trivial.

  • Element Puzzle

    A real puzzle game, based on 4 elements. The levels are very well designed and I felt smart and rewarded every time i completed one. A nice game!

  • Peanball

    A very nice twist on classical flipper! Never seen one like that, beatiful work.

  • Strata

    A little bit repetitive gameplay, but very visually rewarding!

  • Virtual War

    A perfect old-school game, with old-school-like graphics and awesome animations. And FUN to play.
